My dearest Nini,

A hundred days ago, something shifted — quietly, the way the best things always do. I don't know if you felt it too, but I remember exactly how the light looked that day, and how easy it felt to just… be with you.

Since then I've learned the way you laugh when something surprises you, the face you make when the food is good, the exact moment you stop pretending you're not tired. I've been collecting these things without meaning to, the way you collect film photos — small and true and yours.

One hundred days isn't very long. But it's long enough to know I want a hundred more. And then a hundred after that.

Thank you for every single one of them.

forever yours, Bin ♥
